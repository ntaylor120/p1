Who I am and what I do

I am employed by the State of Nebraska and am in transition between my current job of Exchange System Administrator and my preferred job of Website Developer.

I also own my own company (NRTainment Images) and offer my services as website developer/photographer/videographer. My clients include several entertainers, a recording studio, a sailing association and a liquor import company. As a retired performer, I have marketed myself to entertainers, because I understand their requirements for websites, and social media.

Why I am taking this class:

I am taking this class for two reasons:

  • I wish to learn more about dynamic web applications in order to apply that knowledge in my job
  • This course fulfills the requirement for the web development certificate, and the masters program
    I have not yet decided whether to pursue a masters debree. My initial goal is the certificate, but wish to keep my options open.

My programming experience

I have dealt mostly with simple html/css, and some javascript. I took a php programming class through a local community college two years ago, but have not had much opportunity to apply what I learned (and frankly, I've forgotten most of it). I did take the java programming course last semester, and am now applying that in my day job, using java and java server faces to work on existing websites for the state of Nebraska.
